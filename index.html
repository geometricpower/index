Negotiation & next steps

Leverage points with shortlisted vendors

Use competitive tension to improve terms before final award.
Key discussion points
🇳🇬 With NED Consultants
Price: Core ₦74M – probe if any discounts for bundling optional modules (BI, dashboards) at reduced rate.
Support: Year 2-3 support pricing? Current proposal includes only year 1 (with 6mo free). Request fixed rate for extended support.
Dependency: Clarify technical backend reliance on Matiyas – seek independent capability assurance.
Payment: 50% upfront is high; negotiate milestone-based (e.g., 30-30-30-10).
🌍 With Matiyas Solutions
Forex hedge: Propose NGN pricing or fixed USD/NGN rate for duration to eliminate currency risk.
Change request rigidity: Seek capped change fees or a blended hourly rate for minor customisations.
Support flexibility: Request local support option (via NED/Cloudrock) to reduce response time.
Integration: Clarify cost for SCADA/IoT integrations – include ballpark in contract.
📋 General negotiation tactics
  • Avoid price anchoring: Do not treat NED's price as the benchmark since it is already considered high. Maintain competitive tension between both vendors.
  • Negotiate with all vendors: Engage Matiyas and NED(+other vendors) to improve pricing, refine scope, or propose phased implementation to reduce overall cost.
  • Bundle optional modules: If optional modules are required, negotiate a discounted package rather than purchasing them separately later.
  • Reference checks: Speak to 2-3 existing clients of each vendor, preferably local implementations.
  • Service level agreements: Define response times, escalation procedures, and penalties for non-performance.
  • Post-go-live support: Ensure training, documentation, and knowledge transfer are explicitly included in scope.
